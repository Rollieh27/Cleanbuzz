## Introduction ##
Many people have trouble coming up with new dishes they'd like to eat or or drinks they want to try.<br>
Buzz N' Bites contains over 70 categories of food & cocktail recipes which help users find recipes suited to their needs.<br>
We understand that learning new techniques can be frustrating -even overwhelming- for many people.<br>
Our website has many helpful recipes for both novice and experienced chefs & mixologists particularly those interested in exploring new foods or cocktails.<br>

USER STORY: <br>
AS a person exhausted with inflation and deciding which over priced restaurant/bar to visit,<br>
I WANT to find an app where I can search for food and drinks with the ingredients at my disposal<br>
SO THAT i can make myself a good meal.<br>
